Reading list

Books on deck

Curated stacks of formative works drawn from the Western canon. Update the line-up or notes as reading priorities shift.

Classical theology & spirituality

  • Confessions Augustine of Hippo

    Augustine’s prayerful autobiography framing memory, desire, and divine encounter.

  • The Ladder of Divine Ascent John Climacus

    Monastic pedagogy on prayer, imagination, and the ascent toward virtue.

  • Institutes of the Christian Religion John Calvin

    Systematic theology with rich treatments of devotion, scripture, and spiritual formation.

Philosophy & moral imagination

  • Nicomachean Ethics Aristotle

    Virtue ethics and the cultivation of character through habituated practice.

  • Pensées Blaise Pascal

    Fragmentary reflections bridging faith, reason, and the psychology of belief.

  • Leviathan Thomas Hobbes

    Political theology and social imagination amid emerging modernity.

Literature & myth

  • The Divine Comedy Dante Alighieri

    A pilgrimage through the afterlife mapping desire, judgment, and beatific vision.

  • The Iliad Homer

    Epic imagination of honor, grief, and the divine amid war.

  • Paradise Lost John Milton

    Cosmic drama of fall and redemption blending biblical epic with poetic innovation.
