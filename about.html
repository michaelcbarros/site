Mythopoesis in the everyday

Michael C. Barros studies how games, film, and speculative fiction become sites of religious experience and esoteric imagination. His work draws on theology, cultural history, and media analysis to surface the hidden architectures of meaning.

He develops frameworks that help players, readers, and viewers recognize the sacred textures embedded in contemporary storytelling—offering tools for both scholarship and practice.
